Lukas Batema, our founder and CEO, has an impressive repertoire of projects that he's worked on and continues to contribute to. Lukas quickly earned trust within the community, becoming a respected maintainer and collaborator on the official GitHub Repository. With over 35,000 users on the support Discord server and millions using the bot platform-wide, it is a testament to Lukas' commitment to creating innovative ways to interact with AI and Large Language Models (LLMs).
